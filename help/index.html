Top 3 Questions

  • Is World of Cars Online free?
  • What is the World of Cars Online?
  • What if I forget my Account ID or Password?

Answer:

There is no cost for creating a Car, racing with friends, and playing in the World of Cars Online. However, to have access to certain features like advanced levels in mini games, buying items to customize your car, garage and yard, pursuing a professional racing career, and much more, there is a small monthly fee.

The World of Cars Online is a virtual world based on the Disney/Pixar animated feature where kids can create their own Car and join a safe and fun community. From racing, playing games and exploring, to chatting and making friends, The World of Cars Online offers fun activities for kids and peace of mind for parents. There is no third-party advertising and it's free to play but access to special features requires a subscription.

If you forget your Account ID or Password, all you need to do is click here for Account ID or here for Password and follow the instructions on the page.
